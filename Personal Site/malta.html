The Island of Gozo

Gozo:

This is a photo of us at Gozo's iconic "Blue Hole." This spot is a natural rock formation that looks like an upright cylinder. Here, the water is crystal blue and is home to a number of fish and jellyfish species. It is an incredible spot for scuba and free diving.

Interestingly, the wedding between Daenerys and Khol Drogo in Game of Thrones was filmed on Gozo. Gozo is also home to the fabled Calypso's cave from the Odyssey, which we got to visit.

This photo was taken from Calypso's cave and overlooks Gozo's red sand beach.
