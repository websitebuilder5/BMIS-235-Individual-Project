The Beautiful City of Zurich and the Surrounding Countryside

Hiking Through Switzerland:

The highlight of Switzerland was a hike through the hillside. It was a foggy day and we got rained on, but the air was super crisp and refreshing. It felt nice to get outside and away from the cramped and touristy Florence. This is a photo from our hike, where you can see the faint outline of Zurich in the distance.

All throughout the hike we saw cows, sheep, and goats. This is a photo of me at the summit standing by some cows that were protected by an electric fence. I found that out the hard way.
